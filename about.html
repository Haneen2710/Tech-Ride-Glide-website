About TRG

Tech Ride & Glide – Bridging Mobility and Sustainability

Our Mission

At TRG, we believe in creating accessible, eco-friendly transportation solutions tailored for youth and students. Our platform connects users with bikes and skateboards for short-term rentals, promoting greener urban mobility and reducing carbon emissions.

Who We Are

Founded by a team of eco-conscious innovators and engineers, TRG is more than a service – it’s a movement. We're dedicated to empowering the next generation through technology-driven, sustainable travel options.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We embrace cutting-edge tech to simplify mobility.

Sustainability

Our entire model is built around green principles.

Community

We support students and youth to connect with local vendors and each other.

Trust

Security, reliability, and transparency at every step.
